Why People Use FM WhatsApp

Many users are drawn to FM WhatsApp because it provides functionalities that they feel are missing in the official app. The ability to customize the look and feel of the app, combined with enhanced privacy controls, makes FM WhatsApp appealing to those who want a more personalized and secure messaging experience.
